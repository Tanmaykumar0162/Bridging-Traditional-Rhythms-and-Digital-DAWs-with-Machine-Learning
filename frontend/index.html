How It Elevates Your Production


1. Capture the Feel

Stop quantizing the life out of your beats. Our algorithm extracts the original performer's deviations so your drum racks sound authentically played, tracking exactly early/late transient shifts.

2. Instant Arrangements

The AI listens to complex Dha/Dhin strikes and maps them to kicks, snares, hats, and toms using production-standard GM mapping. Instantly drag-and-drop the generated MIDI file into Ableton or Logic.

3. Smart Grid Adaptation

Traditional Taals like Teentaal or Rupak don't always fit neat grids. TablatoDrum understands the meter and forces a sensible 4/4 or 7/8 interpretation without losing the cultural groove.
